About the roll

One camera.
Two cities.
No retakes.

A single roll of disposable film carried through Kolkata and Varanasi. The camera cost less than a meal. The frames are irreplaceable.

India doesn't wait for you to frame it. The light changes before you lift the camera. The crowds move through your shot. The grain isn't noise — it's atmosphere you couldn't have planned.
