About the roll

One roll.
The whole island.

One roll of Kodak 200 pressed into a disposable camera and carried across the South Island of New Zealand — no spare, no second chances.

From the flat Canterbury Plains to the walls of Milford Sound, every frame was a decision. The grain, the underexposed skies, the washed-out teal of the lakes — none of it was corrected. That's what the camera saw.

Aotearoa deserves more than a phone screen.
